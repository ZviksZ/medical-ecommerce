CJSC "Pentcroft Pharma" from the moment of its foundation aims to take care of women's health

The path, which began in 1994 with the work on the synthesis of steroids together with Pharmsintez, led in 2002 to the release of a synthetic drug with antigestagenic activity - Pencrofton (RU-486). To date, Pentcroft Pharma CJSC offers solutions and products for gynecology, obstetrics, andrology and surgery.

Medicinal provision

The portfolio of Pentcroft Pharma CJSC includes more than 50 original medicines, dietary supplements and medical products in the following areas:

  • Medical termination of pregnancy
  • Manual vacuum aspiration
  • Intrauterine contraception
  • Prevention of premature birth
  • Preparation of the cervix and induction of labor
  • Relief of postpartum bleeding
  • Insufficiency of the pelvic floor
  • Infertility
  • Diagnostic tests
Development of science and research

CJSC Pentcroft Pharma cooperates with Russian research centers and specialized departments of universities in the field of clinical research of innovative methods of diagnosis, prevention and treatment.
